About Us

The Master Plan…

After years of experience gained working in industry, we wanted a change from the corporate world and sought out a new challenge working with entrepreneurial businesses. It was at this point that we realised that most small businesses we talked to were severely lacking access to qualified accounting professionals. There are three key factors driving this.

  • ● Qualified accountants command high salaries which most SME’s simply cannot absorb into their business

  • ● Small businesses are unlikely to provide sufficient workload to keep a full time accountant busy - it just isn’t economic to be paying an accountants wages to cover simple administrative tasks

  • ● Most small business owners are entrepreneurial by nature and therefore do not come from a financial background. Because of this they can be unaware of the added value a skilled accountant can bring to their business

  • ● Regular Visits – we won’t just visit your office for a coffee and chat once a quarter, we believe regular visits are vital so you get to know us and always feel like we are available for advice and guidance

  • ● More than just the basics – we’re more than happy to do the basics, but we won’t stop there. Business plan’s, cash flows, negotiating with lenders will all be part of the service

  • ● Affordable fixed fees – we will agree these at the outset and because they are fixed, you won’t have to worry about us charging you for that 15 minutes we spent replying to your email

We found that SME’s therefore often just employ qualified accountants to help with the basics, being year-end accounts and tax returns. Although few clients have complained of a lack of choice in these areas, most do complain that the services provided are expensive, impersonal and often uninformative. Spotting an opportunity, we have developed a flexible service, which is a fraction of the cost of employing a full-time accountant and offers far more than the typical one visit a year accountancy practice:

Our Values

Being a firm of Chartered Accountants, our values are
closely aligned with those of the ICAEW. As a fast growing practice,
we are dedicated to shaping our business around these core values.

Mutually Beneficial Relationships

Develop long term relationships that benefit all parties involved. Ensure that the needs of our clients are at the forefront of everything we do.

Honesty & Integrity

Be straightforward and truthful in all our dealings. Willing to take on board new ideas, information and suggestions. Do not misrepresent ourselves or associate ourselves with misleading or false information.

Objectivity

Always have regard to the facts and considerations relevant to the task at hand. Never allow bias, self-interest or personal gain to override our actions.

Professional Competence

Ensure compliance with applicable laws, regulations and standards; Constantly strive to enhance our skills, knowledge and experience as required to provide a professional service.

Respect

Behave in a professional manner at all times. Encourage an inclusive working environment where others are treated with dignity and respect.

Services

We offer a broad range of financial and accountancy services to all of our clients. An example of some of the services on offer are given below:

Business Planning

  • ● Specific experience in business recovery and turnaround strategies.

  • ● Production of specialised and precise rolling cash flow models.

  • ● Creation of meaningful budgets and detailed forecasts to assist with management decisions.

  • ● Introduction to and negotiation with potential lenders to promote business growth.

  • ● Financial modelling bringing clients business plans to life.

Business Support

  • ● Provision of accurate management information, tailored to each client based on management needs.

  • ● Payroll processing and advice.

  • ● Negotiation with HMRC regarding time to pay arrangements.

  • ● Statutory accounts prepared by ICAEW qualified accounting professionals, providing the highest level of integrity.

Recruitment

As a relatively new practice, we have enjoyed rapid growth, both in terms of fees and the number of clients we work with. We recognise that this can only be maintained by continuing to recruit and keep hold of outstanding candidates that are willing to go above and beyond expectations.

We know you don’t become an experienced accountant overnight, so that’s why we are committed to providing a learning environment that all our employees can benefit from. To enable us to do this, we have registered as an ICAEW Authorised Training Employer (ATE office No: L004248272) enabling us to help train the future Chartered Accountants of this world! As an employer we also provide support and financial assistance to employees studying towards the Association of Accounting Technician’s (AAT) qualification.
